Adjusting room size and shape

Click any wall to enter exact length, then hit enter (or tab) to accept, and move to next wall. Note. You can use "23", or 23', or 23.5' or 23' 6".
Or, left-click and drag wall to desired size.
To change measurement units to metric, select "cm" option below.
To adjust corners, check Lock Corners option below, then drag corner to new position.
Click on floor area to see current room dimensions.
Use on-screen Zoom controls, mouse wheel or "pinch" to adjust zoom view.
